Math Facts is a free, ad-free, tracking-free way to practice math facts. There's no time pressure and no harsh response for wrong answers. All data is stored in your local browser only. - Complete 40 problems to earn a star each day. - Complete extra problems for a silver or gold star. - Click the operation to toggle (+, -, x, รท). - Click the Calendar to see your recent work and streak. - Click the Speedometer to see your speed per problem. - Click the Checkmark to see your accuracy per problem. Please report any issues at https://github.com/ScottLouvau/MathFacts. Send any feedback to scottlo@outlook.com.
